My Story

I am Marjan Habibi, a self-taught web developer and the founder of Women Family Empower, an initiative focused on supporting women and families through education, awareness, and empowerment. When girls’ schools were closed in Afghanistan, I refused to let silence shape my future.

Through online learning, discipline, and resilience, I developed technical skills in web development and discovered the power of technology as a voice for change and self-expression.

My long-term goal is to become a neurosurgeon and a writer who tells the stories of Afghan women — stories of pain, resistance, and hope — and proves that even under restriction, dreams can grow and create real impact.
